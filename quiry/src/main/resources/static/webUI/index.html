University of Toronto

The University of Toronto is a public research university in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was founded by royal charter in 1827 as King's College, the first institution of higher learning in the colony of Upper Canada.

U of T Scarborough

The University of Toronto Scarborough (UTSC), based in the Scarborough district of Toronto, Ontario, Canada. It was established in 1964 as Scarborough College, a constituent college of the Faculty of Arts and Science. The college expanded into a mid-sized university following its designation as an autonomic division of the university in 1972.

Quiry

What We Support?

Sharing

We allow instructors and students upload documents that can help for academic study, which that can be include lecture notes, study notes, past exams, academic papers...ect., and we can also support txt, pdf, html files. In addition, instructor can also restrict he/she's uploaded course information to it's own class.

Download

We open our data space for EVERYONE(not include the restricted files), especially for the guest who's do not have an account to log in, they can also download documents that are open sources.

Searching

EVERYONE can search using the search engine we provided. We use the fastest method for seperate different kind of search. Filter option is provided in our search engine.

Free

All the sources in our site is consider "FREE"! No one need to pay any money. We stands on the student side, we wouldn't want any student to pay money to get the study note.
